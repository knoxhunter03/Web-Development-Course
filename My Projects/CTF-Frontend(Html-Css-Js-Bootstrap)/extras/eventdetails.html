What is
#CYPERQUEST

Cyper Quest 2023 is an electrifying Capture The Flag (CTF) event organized by the Cyper Club of the CSE - Cloud Technology and Information Security Department.

Immerse yourself in the realms of cybersecurity, where you'll tackle challenges such as Web Hacking, Cloud Hacking, OSINT, Forensic, and Cryptography. This event is designed to offer you a dynamic platform to enhance your practical cybersecurity skills, placing a strong emphasis on your growth and development in this exciting field.
