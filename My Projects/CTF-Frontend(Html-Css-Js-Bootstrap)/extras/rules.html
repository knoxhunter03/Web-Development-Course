#Rules

  1. Team Size: Each team can consist of a maximum of 4 members. Solo participants are not welcomed, please join a team.
  2. Fair Play: Participants are expected to demonstrate fair play, sportsmanship, and ethical behavior throughout the competition. Any form of cheating or unethical conduct will result in immediate disqualification.
  3. Eligibility: Cyper Quest is open to [Specify the eligibility criteria, e.g., students, professionals, etc.]. Participants must adhere to the specified eligibility requirements.
  4. Registration: All participants must register in advance through the official Cyper Quest website. Late registrations may not be accepted.
  5. Scoring: Scoring will be based on the successful completion of challenges. Each challenge will have a specific point value, and the team or individual with the highest score at the end of the event will be declared the winner.
  6. Flag Submission: Teams must submit the flag (solution) for each challenge through the designated platform. Correct flag submissions will earn points.
  7. Time Limits: Challenges may have time limits. Participants must complete the challenges within the specified time frame to earn points.
  8. Code of Conduct: Participants are expected to maintain a respectful and professional demeanor throughout the event. Harassment, discrimination, or any form of inappropriate behavior will not be tolerated.
